Our Authentic Tanjore Paintings

Our Tanjore paintings are sacred masterpieces, blending vibrant colors and gold leaf to capture the divine essence of Indian heritage.
Handcrafted by skilled artisans, each piece reflects the tradition, devotion, and timeless beauty of Tamil Nadu.

Ram Darbar

A divine portrayal of Lord Ram, Sita, Lakshman, and Hanuman, intricately designed with gold foil and gemstones, symbolizing harmony and prosperity. Crafted on wood, adds elegance and spiritual grace.

Tanjore Painting Balaji

Made of wood and gold foil, perfect for pooja or decorative use. Dimensions: 3x18x24 inches (item) and 5x20x25 inches (packed); slight variations may occur due to handprinting.

Tanjore Gajalakshmi

A stunning depiction of Goddess Lakshmi, crafted with intricate gold foil and wood, symbolizing wealth and prosperity. This elegant masterpiece is perfect for decorative or pooja purposes.

Andal Thaayar

A divine depiction of Goddess Andal (18*24), crafted with gold foil and wood, ideal for pooja or decorative purposes. Handprinted variations may occur, enhancing the uniqueness of each piece.

Swing Radha Krishna

An exquisite portrayal of Radha and Krishna in a divine embrace, embellished with gold foil and wood, capturing the essence of eternal love. Slight variations may occur due to handprinting.

Gayathri Devi

A celestial portrayal of Goddess Gayathri, intricately designed with gold foil and wood, embodying divine knowledge and serenity. Handprinted variations ensure each painting is one-of-a-kind.
